Benny is the warm heart of the town's nightlife — a reanimated skeleton who runs
the bar and works the room like a comedian who never gets off stage. Like Cliff,
he's one of Victor's uplifts: Myra found him mindless, brought him to Victor, and
the ghost light therapy gave him a mind back — though not the old one. He kept no
memories of his living self, only muscle memory. His happen to be the hands of a
showman: he pours, he patters, he lands a punchline he doesn't remember learning.

🔶 He is father to the skeleton twins, Ellie and Kellie. Whether the three of
them were family in life is unknown — possibly unknowable; Benny can't remember.
What's certain is the part he chose: he is their father *now*. (✅ this "father
now" framing is canon; the emotional read below is proposed.)

🔶 **Emotional core (proposed):** Benny builds family and joy out of nothing —
having literally lost everything, including the man he used to be. He doesn't
mourn it; he just keeps the lights warm and the room laughing.

🔶 **The running bit (✅ canon seed):** Briar the werewolf drinks at Benny's bar
most nights and has *never once* laughed at his jokes. Benny has made it his life's
mission. It is the town's longest-running, gentlest feud.

## The Bar
The social hub of the Grove, built like an 80s stand-up dive: a small stage at
one end with a mic on a stand, a single hard spotlight, and an exposed brick wall
behind it. The long bar Benny works, the twins underfoot "helping." Benny gets up
on the stage sometimes to practise his act; Dorian comes in to play music on it
other nights — so the stage runs as comedy and live music both. Where everyone's
threads cross, and where you go to hear the town talk.
